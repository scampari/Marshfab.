About Marsh Fab

Sam Perez

Owner | Designer | Builder

Sam is a Southeast Louisiana native coming from a long line of fishermen, craftsmen, and builders. Sam has been a builder for nearly a decade, first in residential construction, then making small pieces for friends and family, and later working as a finisher and converting a small bus, all while maintaining a fulfilling career behind the bar. Sam opened the doors to Marsh Fabrications in the fall of 2020 seeking to turn a long time passion into a full time career.

Sam is a trained welder and self taught woodworker. Their years of experience behind the bar reflects itself in their ergonomic design. Sam hopes to combine these two worlds by creating timeless pieces that would be at home in any bar or living space.
